What Is Hip-Hop?

“ Hip-Hop is a music genre developed in the United States by inner city African Americans in the 1970’s. Which consists of a stylized rhythmic music that commonly accompanies rap. Rap music developed as part of hip-hop culture; a subculture stylized by four key components:MCing/rapping,DJing/ scratching turntables, breakdancing, and graffiti writing. Another component that came along was beatboxing.”
